About Shurokha

Shurokha is a platform dedicated to supporting pregnant women and new mothers. Our mission is to empower mothers with reliable health information, easy access to healthcare professionals, and tools to track their health and their baby’s growth. We believe every mother deserves the best care, guidance, and support throughout her journey.
